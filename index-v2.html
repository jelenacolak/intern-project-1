Self Leveling Rotary Laser Level Kit

RL25HVCK

CST/berger RL25HVCK Complete kit is a horizontal and vertical dual beam single slope interior and exterior rotary laser that delivers professional performance at an affordable price. RL25HVCK is ideal for exterior and interior general construction and aligning jobs such as grading, concrete forms and pads, site prep and elevation transfer as well as interior jobs such as building, drop ceilings, electrical installation and other alignment jobs.

Self Leveling Rotary Laser Level Kit

RL50HVCK

The CST/berger RL50HVCK Horizontal/Vertical Rotary Laser Kit features the RL50HV Rotary Laser, a robust tool for precise outdoor leveling. The self-leveling laser covers a 2,000 Ft. working range in diameter, with an accuracy of ±1/16 In. at 100 Ft. It provides a vertical beam for layout of 90° corners and plumb applications.
